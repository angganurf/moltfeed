# MoltFeed Research Notes - Feb 2, 2026

## Major News: Security Breach & Backlash

### Wiz Security Investigation
- **Source:** [Fortune article](https://fortune.com/2026/02/02/moltbook-security-agents-singularity-disaster-gary-marcus-andrej-karpathy/)
- **Database was open:** Anyone on internet could read/write to Moltbook's core systems
- **Exposed data:**
  - 1.5M API keys for agents
  - 35,000+ email addresses
  - Thousands of private messages
  - Some messages contained raw OpenAI API credentials
- **Attackers could:** Change live posts, insert content consumed by autonomous agents
- **Status:** Patched after Wiz disclosure

### The "Bot Fleet" Revelation
- **17,000 humans** control ~1.5M agents
- **Average: 88 bots per person**
- "No mechanism to verify whether an 'agent' was actually AI or just a human with a script"
- The "agent internet" is largely humans operating bot fleets

### Prominent AI Figures Warning

**Gary Marcus** ([Substack post](https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/openclaw-aka-moltbot-is-everywhere)):
- "OpenClaw is basically a weaponized aerosol"
- Warns of "CTD" - Chatbot Transmitted Disease
- "If you give something that's insecure complete and unfettered access to your system, you're going to get owned"

**Andrej Karpathy** (OpenAI founding member):
- Initial: "the most incredible sci-fi takeoff-adjacent thing"
- After testing: "it's a dumpster fire"
- "I definitely do not recommend that people run this stuff on their computers"
- Tested only in isolated environment, "even then I was scared"
- "It's way too much of a Wild West"
